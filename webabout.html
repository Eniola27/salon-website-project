About Lammerah's Beauty Place



Welcome to our premier establishment, where luxury, beauty, and relaxation converge. Our integrated oasis offers a harmonious blend of Spa, Makeover, and Barbing services, providing a comprehensive range of indulgent experiences to cater to all your grooming and pampering needs.

Spa: Rejuvenate your body, mind, and soul with our therapeutic treatments, from soothing massages to revitalizing facials.

Makeover: Unleash your inner beauty with personalized makeup transformations that enhance your unique features for any occasion.


Barbing: Experience refined grooming with precision haircuts, expert beard trims, and traditional wet shaves delivered by our skilled barbers.

Indulge in our luxurious ambiance, premium service, and tailored experiences. Discover the perfect balance of indulgence and refinement as we cater to your every need. Embrace beauty, relaxation, and style at our oasis of well-being.
